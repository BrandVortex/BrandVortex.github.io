About

Find Out More About Us

Welcome to the "About Us" section of the Indian Red Cross Society's website. Here, we provide an overview of our history, mission, values, and the dedicated individuals who drive our humanitarian efforts.

Established in 1920, the Indian Red Cross Society (IRCS) has a rich legacy of service to humanity.

Our mission is to prevent and alleviate human suffering by mobilizing the power of humanity. We achieve this by providing vital services in disaster response, healthcare, blood donation, and community development.

  • We believe in the inherent dignity of every person and our duty to help those in need, without discrimination.

    We do not take sides in conflicts, ensuring access to our services for all parties involved.

  • We maintain our autonomy to act in accordance with our principles and the best interests of those we serve.

    Our work is powered by volunteers, who selflessly give their time and skills to support our humanitarian mission.
